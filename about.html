About Grandeur Resort

Discover our story of luxury and excellence

Our Story

Since 1992, Grandeur Resort has been synonymous with luxury, elegance, and unparalleled service. Nestled on the pristine, silver-sand shores of Malindi, our resort has welcomed discerning travelers from around the world who seek the ultimate in comfort and sophistication, all with a touch of authentic Kenyan hospitality.

What began as a vision to create a sanctuary where guests could escape the ordinary has evolved into one of Kenya's most celebrated luxury destinations. Every detail of our resort, from our architecture inspired by rich Swahili culture to our world-class amenities, reflects our commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To provide an extraordinary experience that exceeds expectations, creating lasting memories through exceptional service, luxurious accommodations, and genuine Kenyan hospitality.

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for perfection in every aspect of our service, ensuring that each guest experience surpasses expectations.

Sustainability

We are committed to preserving the natural beauty of the Kenyan coast for future generations to enjoy.

Authenticity

We celebrate and share the rich Swahili culture and Kenyan heritage with our guests.

Community

We support our local community and believe in giving back to the place we call home.

Our Location

Grandeur Resort is perfectly positioned on the stunning Malindi coastline, offering guests immediate access to pristine beaches, vibrant local markets, and renowned seafood restaurants. Our prime location provides the perfect balance of secluded luxury and convenient access to Kenya's most celebrated coastal attractions.

  • 10 minutes from Vasco da Gama Pillar
  • 15 minutes from Malindi Marine National Park
  • 20 minutes from the Falconry of Kenya
  • 30 minutes from the Gedi Ruins
  • Direct beach access
  • Panoramic Indian Ocean views
